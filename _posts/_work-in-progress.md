+ 1625 Jean Hogue is the farthest ancestor
+ His son, Pierre, born in France, is the first to move to Canada. He marries a native Canadian but she dies early and her sons also die young. None of those descendants are in our ancestry proper. He second wife Jeanne Theodore has 9 children with Pierre and one with another man after having 7 first with Pierre.
