## About

Not much is known about our earliest ancestor. Born in France, Jean marries Nicole Dubus at age 21 while she is about 19. They have one child that we know of.


## Notes

+ **Date of Death** — Ancestry.com lists his death as 10 November 1676 in Montreal, Canada (source unknown)
+ **Nicole Dubus** — Ancestry.com lists her death as 4 May 1664 in Picardie, France (source unknown)
+ **Children** — If there are other children from the marriage of Jean and Nicole, they are unknown at the time of this writing.
+ **Nicole’s surname** — Some sources have Nicole’s surname as “DuBusset.” (Generation 11.32) Marcel decided to use the most commonly used variation of “Dubus” for continuity purposes.
+ Typically, the location of Canada is shown for familiarity purposes. At this time in Canadian history, it would have been known as “Nouvelle France” (New France). See (Generation 11.32) Marcel’s “Introduction” and “Story of Pierre” for additional information. _New France became the British Province of Quebec in 1763 after France ceded it in the Treaty of Paris at the end of the Seven Years’ War._
